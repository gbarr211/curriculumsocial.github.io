Welcome to Curriculum Social – where learning happens through real connections. We bring together families, experienced educators, and innovative tools to create an educational experience that truly fits your child and your life.

Our Educational Philosophy

  • Develop at their own pace
  • Build practical life skills alongside academic knowledge

Parent Involvement Opportunities

At Curriculum Social, we believe parents are their children's first and most important teachers. Our platform offers multiple ways for you to be involved in your child's educational journey.

The Parent-Teacher Model

Share your expertise by becoming a parent-teacher! Our unique model allows parents to teach in our community while receiving tuition credits.

How It Works:

  • 4 hours teaching weekly = 50% tuition reduction
  • 8+ hours teaching weekly = 100% tuition waiver
  • Additional compensation available beyond tuition offset
  • Performance bonuses for highly-rated sessions

Eligibility:

  • Subject matter expertise or professional credentials
  • Completion of our teaching methodology training
  • Background verification and safety checks
  • Commitment to our educational philosophy

Fair Evaluation System

We've developed a multi-source feedback system to ensure quality teaching while preventing conflicts of interest. Parent-teachers never evaluate their own children, and regular external assessments maintain objectivity.

Host Learning Activities

Offer your home as a learning venue for small group activities. Host families receive credits and get to shape the learning environment.

Organize Field Trips

Share your local knowledge by organizing and leading educational field trips to interesting and educational locations.

Curriculum Contribution

Help evaluate and improve our curriculum through our collaborative voting system and feedback tools.

Safety First Framework

Your child's safety is our absolute priority. We've built comprehensive safety protocols into every aspect of our platform.

Two-Adult Supervision Policy

  • All learning activities require two adults present at all times
  • Clear documentation of any rare exceptions for emergencies
  • Learning environments designed for visibility and safety
  • Teachers paired with complementary skills for enhanced learning

Comprehensive Verification

  • Background checks for all teachers and regular adult participants
  • Venue safety inspections and certifications
  • Regular renewal of verification credentials
  • Community vouching system supplements formal checks

Monitored Environment

  • Clear designation of supervised learning zones
  • Privacy-conscious monitoring of common areas only
  • Optional livestream access for parents during activities
  • Scheduled in-person observation opportunities

Community Accountability

  • Anonymous reporting system for any concerns
  • Quarterly safety reviews of venues and protocols
  • Rotating parent/teacher safety committee
  • Clear incident response protocols

Getting Started with Curriculum Social

Joining our community is simple. Here's how to begin your family's journey with us:

1

Profile Creation

Create your family profile, indicating your educational philosophy, children's interests and needs, and your location preferences.

  • Complete interest surveys to help us understand your preferences
  • Set your location and preferred activity radius
  • Indicate any special interests or needs
2

Matching & Connection

Our system will match you with compatible families, teachers, and learning opportunities in your area.

  • Review potential matches and express interest
  • Connect through our secure messaging system
  • Schedule initial meet-ups in safe, public settings
3

Learning Path Creation

Work with our system to design a personalized learning path for each child, blending AI-guided, teacher-led, and social learning opportunities.

  • Select curriculum focus areas based on interests
  • Schedule regular activities and sessions
  • Choose your level of parent participation
4

Begin Your Journey

Start with an orientation session to familiarize yourself with the platform, then dive into your personalized learning experience.

  • Attend a virtual or in-person orientation
  • Set up your child's learning dashboard
  • Join your first activities and begin learning!
