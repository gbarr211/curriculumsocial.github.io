1. PURPOSE AND OVERVIEW

This Agreement establishes the terms under which Curriculum Social will utilize the Venue Partner's space for educational activities, including but not limited to classes, workshops, learning sessions, and community events. This partnership aims to create safe, engaging learning environments for children while providing value to both Parties.

  • Clean, well-maintained environment suitable for children
  • Comfortable temperature control (heating/cooling as needed)
  • Accessible restroom facilities
  • Compliance with local fire safety regulations
  • Clearly marked emergency exits
  • First aid kit accessible on premises

2.3 Additional Desirable Features

The following features are preferred but not required:

Access to outdoor space
Natural lighting
Storage space for educational materials
Internet access
Whiteboard or wall space for displays
Water fountain or drinking water access

5. SAFETY STANDARDS AND MONITORING

5.1 Two-Adult Policy

Curriculum Social implements a strict two-adult policy where at least two adults will be present during all activities involving children. The Venue Partner acknowledges this policy and agrees not to interfere with its implementation.

5.2 Designated Learning Areas

Specific areas within the venue will be designated for learning activities. These areas must have appropriate visibility while maintaining a productive learning environment. Curriculum Social and the Venue Partner will agree on these designated areas before activities begin.

5.3 Safety Inspections

Curriculum Social reserves the right to conduct safety inspections of the venue before beginning the partnership and periodically thereafter. The Venue Partner agrees to remedy any identified safety concerns within a reasonable timeframe.

5.4 Monitoring and Supervision

The Venue Partner agrees to the following monitoring arrangements to ensure child safety:

  • Open visibility in all learning areas
  • No private or completely enclosed spaces for activities
  • Clear boundaries for children regarding which areas of the venue they may access

6. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY

6.1 Insurance Requirements

Both parties agree to maintain appropriate insurance coverage:

  • Curriculum Social will maintain liability insurance covering its educational activities with minimum coverage of _______.
  • The Venue Partner will maintain property insurance and general liability insurance with minimum coverage of _______.

6.2 Proof of Insurance

Both parties will provide certificates of insurance to the other party upon request and will notify the other party of any material changes to insurance coverage.

6.3 Liability Limitations

Curriculum Social assumes responsibility for the actions of its staff, teachers, and participating students during scheduled activities. The Venue Partner assumes responsibility for maintaining a safe physical environment. Neither party shall be liable for the negligent acts of the other party.

7. VENUE PREPARATION AND MAINTENANCE

7.1 Setup and Cleanup

Curriculum Social will:

  • Arrive _____ minutes before scheduled activities for setup
  • Return the space to its original condition after use
  • Remove all materials and equipment brought for activities
  • Report any damages or issues immediately

7.2 Venue Partner Responsibilities

The Venue Partner will:

  • Ensure the space is clean and ready for use at the scheduled time
  • Maintain functional facilities (restrooms, electricity, water, etc.)
  • Address any maintenance issues promptly
  • Notify Curriculum Social of any changes to the venue that might affect activities

8. BRANDING AND PROMOTIONAL CONSIDERATIONS

8.1 Co-Branding

Curriculum Social may display temporary signage during activities. Permanent signage or branding requires prior written agreement from the Venue Partner.

8.2 Cross-Promotion

The parties agree to the following cross-promotional activities:

  • Curriculum Social may list the venue as an official partner location in its materials
  • The Venue Partner may promote its relationship with Curriculum Social
  • Both parties will refer to the other positively in relevant communications

8.3 Photography and Media

Curriculum Social may take photographs or videos of activities for promotional purposes, excluding any identifying features of the venue not previously approved. The Venue Partner grants permission for such media use provided it portrays the venue in a positive light.

9. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information shared during this partnership, including but not limited to:

  • Educational methodologies and curriculum details
  • Business plans and financial information
  • Personal information of participants
  • Venue Partner's proprietary business information

This confidentiality obligation survives the termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years.

10. TERM AND TERMINATION

10.1 Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for a period of _____ months/years, after which it will automatically renew for successive _____ month/year periods unless either party provides notice of non-renewal.

10.2 Termination with Notice

Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause by providing _____ days' written notice to the other party.

10.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:

  • Materially breaches any term of this Agreement
  • Engages in illegal activity
  • Creates unsafe conditions
  • Becomes insolvent or ceases operations

10.4 Post-Termination Obligations

Upon termination, Curriculum Social will remove all materials from the venue within _____ days. Any outstanding payments will be settled within _____ days of termination.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

11.1 Good Faith Resolution

The Parties agree to attempt to resolve any disputes through good faith discussions before pursuing formal resolution methods.

11.2 Mediation

If disputes cannot be resolved through discussion, the Parties agree to seek mediation before pursuing litigation. The cost of mediation will be shared equally between the Parties.
