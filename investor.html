Executive Summary & Vision

Curriculum Social is a hybrid educational platform that bridges the gap between traditional homeschooling and formal education by combining:

  • AI-powered personalized learning that adapts to each student's needs and interests
  • Human teaching expertise from qualified educators and knowledgeable parents
  • Community-based social learning that connects like-minded families

Our mission is to provide high-quality, flexible education for globally mobile families and digital nomads who are underserved by traditional educational systems.

Market Opportunity

Growing Digital Nomad Family Segment

The digital nomad population has grown by over 130% since 2019, with 35 million worldwide. Of these, approximately 15-20% are families with school-aged children seeking flexible, high-quality education.

Traditional options are limited to:

  • International schools ($15,000-30,000 annually) - fixed location, inflexible
  • Online schools - lack social interaction and local integration
  • Self-directed homeschooling - high parent burden, inconsistent quality

This creates a significant gap for a solution that offers quality, flexibility, and affordability.

Koh Samui: Initial Target Market

  • 500-800 expatriate/digital nomad families with children
  • High turnover rate (40-60% annually) creating need for flexible education
  • Limited international school options (3 schools, $10,000+ annually)
  • Growing homeschool community lacking structured resources

Risk Analysis & Mitigation

Key Risks

Slower Student Acquisition

Extended cash burn period if growth metrics fall below projections.

Higher Tech Costs

AI and data storage costs could exceed estimates, reducing margins.

Teacher Retention Issues

Quality inconsistency, increased recruitment costs if turnover is high.

Competition from Traditional Schools

Price pressure and user acquisition challenges from established institutions.

Mitigation Strategies

Phased Launch Approach

Focus on smaller geographic areas with tiered feature rollout to control costs.

Intelligent Resource Allocation

Data retention policies and usage-based resource scaling to control costs.

Strong Parent-Teacher Model

Create community ownership and competitive compensation to retain talent.

Unique Positioning Strategy

Focus on transient populations underserved by traditional education options.
