Our Hybrid Learning Model

Combining the Best of All Worlds

Curriculum Social integrates three powerful learning approaches:

  • AI-Powered Learning: Personalized curriculum adapted to each student's unique needs and interests
  • Human Teaching: Expert instruction from qualified educators and knowledge-sharing parent-teachers
  • Community Learning: Social development through meaningful in-person activities and collaborations

This balanced approach ensures comprehensive educational development while building lasting community connections.

Safety First Approach

At Curriculum Social, child safety is our highest priority. Our comprehensive safety framework ensures:

Verified Participants

Background checks for all adults involved in education

Multiple Adult Supervision

All activities supervised by at least two qualified adults

Safe Venues

Carefully inspected and certified learning environments

Our strict protocols and transparent policies create a secure environment where children can thrive.

Starting in Beautiful Koh Samui

Our initial community is launching in Koh Samui, Thailand - an international hub perfect for our vision of connected learning.

Our initial focus areas include:

  • Chaweng - Eastern coast, home to international schools and family amenities
  • Lamai - Southeastern coast, with growing expat and digital nomad communities
  • Bophut - Northern coast, featuring Fisherman's Village and family-friendly facilities
  • Maenam - Northwestern coast, quieter area with established coworking spaces

With community hubs throughout the island, convenient access is available wherever you're located.
