Interest Disclosure Agreement

This Interest Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the date of signature below between Curriculum Social ("Company") and the undersigned individual or entity ("Recipient").

1. Purpose of Discussions

The purpose of this Agreement is to protect confidential information that may be disclosed during exploratory discussions and preliminary evaluations related to the Company's educational platform that combines AI-powered learning, human teaching, and community-based social learning ("Purpose").

2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by the Company to Recipient, either directly or indirectly, in writing, orally or by inspection of tangible objects, including without limitation: business plans, financial projections, technical specifications, educational methodologies, algorithms, matching systems, safety protocols, user interface designs, pricing structures, and other business information that is marked "confidential" or would reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

3. Permitted Use of Information

Recipient agrees to use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose and not for any other purpose, including, without limitation, competing with the Company or developing similar services. Recipient shall hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent from the Company. Recipient may disclose Confidential Information only to those of its employees or advisors who need to know such information for the Purpose and who have agreed to maintain confidentiality.

4. Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of six (6) months thereafter, Recipient agrees not to solicit for employment or contract work any employee, contractor, partner, or affiliate of the Company with whom Recipient becomes acquainted through discussions related to the Purpose, without prior written consent from the Company.

5. Term & Termination

This Agreement shall remain in effect for six (6) months from the date of signature. The obligations of confidentiality and non-use shall survive any termination of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years from the date of termination.

6. No Obligation

Nothing in this Agreement obligates either party to proceed with any transaction or relationship. This Agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship.

7. Return of Materials

Upon the Company's request, Recipient shall promptly return all tangible materials containing Confidential Information and destroy any copies, notes, or other documents containing Confidential Information.
