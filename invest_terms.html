Governance

Protective Provisions:

The approval of investors holding a majority of the Preferred Stock will be required for:

  • Amendments to the Certificate of Incorporation that adversely affect investor rights
  • Creation of new securities senior to or pari passu with the Preferred Stock
  • Increase or decrease in the authorized number of Board members
  • Sale or liquidation of the Company
  • Incurring debt over $[DEBT_THRESHOLD]

Voting Rights:

The Preferred Stock will vote with the Common Stock on an as-converted basis, plus special voting rights on matters affecting the Preferred Stock.

Drag-Along Rights:

If holders of at least [DRAG_THRESHOLD]% of Common Stock and Preferred Stock (voting together) approve a sale of the Company, all shareholders will be required to participate in such transaction.
