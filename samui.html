Curriculum Social - Koh Samui Launch Plan

Strategic plan for initial market entry in Koh Samui

Market Analysis - Koh Samui Education Landscape

Current Educational Options:

  • International Schools: 4-5 options with annual tuition ranging from $8,000-$20,000
  • Homeschooling Community: Approximately 30-50 families, loosely organized
  • Digital Nomad Families: Growing population with inconsistent educational approaches
  • Thai Public Schools: Limited English instruction, minimal expatriate enrollment

Market Gap: There is currently no solution that combines quality education standards with flexibility, affordability, and social connection for mobile families.

Target Population: Estimated 300-500 potential family users within Koh Samui including permanent expatriates, digital nomads, and education-focused Thai families.

Implementation Timeline

Months 1-2

Foundation Phase

  • Establish legal entity and necessary permits
  • Recruit 3-5 local educators for content development
  • Identify and secure initial venue partnerships
  • Develop localized curriculum framework
  • Create initial marketing materials
Months 3-4

Beta Testing Phase

  • Recruit 15-25 beta test families
  • Launch limited platform functionality
  • Begin weekly in-person activities
  • Implement feedback collection system
  • Host first community showcase event
Months 5-6

Refinement Phase

  • Iterate platform based on beta feedback
  • Expand venue partnerships across island
  • Develop formal parent-teacher training program
  • Create marketing content featuring beta testimonials
  • Build partnerships with local businesses for field trips
Months 7-8

Full Launch Phase

  • Official public launch with tiered pricing
  • Implement complete matching system
  • Expand curriculum offerings across all age groups
  • Host island-wide educational showcase event
  • Begin collection of expansion metrics

Venue Partnership Strategy

Priority Venues:

  1. Mantra Work Lounge (Maenam) - Established coworking with garden space
  2. Desk+Chair Samui (Bophut) - 24-hour coworking with private rooms
  3. International School of Samui (Chaweng) - Weekend/after-hours access
  4. The Library (Chaweng Beach) - Premium venue for special events
  5. Verified Host Homes (Various) - Parent-provided spaces with certification
