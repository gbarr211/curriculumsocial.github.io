Partnership Benefits Summary

Venue Partner Benefits

  • Revenue Generation: Regular bookings with predictable scheduling
  • Off-Peak Utilization: Activities planned during typical low-usage periods
  • Marketing Exposure: Featured on platform and materials as educational partner
  • Community Positioning: Association with innovative educational initiative
  • Network Expansion: Connection to local and expatriate families
  • Minimal Disruption: Structured, supervised activities with clear guidelines
  • Flexible Compensation: Multiple models available (hourly rental, revenue share, etc.)

Venue Safety Standards

Safety Requirements for Venue Partners

  • Visibility: Open areas with good visibility; no enclosed spaces without windows
  • Access Control: Secure entry/exit with monitoring capabilities
  • Emergency Procedures: Clear evacuation routes and first aid availability
  • Cleanliness: Regular cleaning protocols, especially in areas used by children
  • Internet Safety: Secure WiFi with optional content filtering for educational use
  • Physical Safety: No significant hazards; child-appropriate furniture and facilities

Compensation Models

Model Description Best For
Hourly Rental Standard hourly rate with volume discount for regular bookings Coworking spaces with established pricing
Package Booking Discounted rate for regular weekly time slots booked in advance Venues seeking predictable revenue
Revenue Share Percentage of membership fees from activities hosted at venue Partners interested in growing with the platform
Membership Exchange Cross-promotion of services between venue and platform Venues seeking broader community exposure
