TEACHER AGREEMENT

This Teacher Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the date of signature below (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

Curriculum Social, with primary place of business at [Address] ("Curriculum Social" or "Company"); and

________________________________________________ ("Teacher"), residing at _______________________________________________________.

The Company and Teacher may be individually referred to as "Party" and collectively as "Parties."

1. SERVICES PROVIDED

Teacher agrees to provide the following educational services (the "Services") to Curriculum Social:

  • Deliver educational instruction and facilitation aligned with Curriculum Social's Montessori-inspired approach and global standards
  • Prepare lesson materials and learning activities appropriate for assigned student groups
  • Provide constructive feedback and assessments on student progress
  • Participate in community-building educational events and activities
  • Maintain appropriate documentation of student participation and progress
  • Collaborate with other teachers and parents in the educational community
  • Adhere to all safety protocols and educational standards of Curriculum Social

The specific schedule, subjects, and student groups will be arranged separately and may be updated by mutual agreement of the Parties.

2. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE

Compensation will be structured as follows, depending on Teacher's classification:

2.1 External Teacher Compensation

External Teachers will be compensated at the rate of $[20-30] per teaching hour, with preparation time compensated at [50%] of the teaching rate. Performance bonuses of up to [20%] may be awarded based on evaluation criteria outlined in Section 8. Payment will be made [monthly/bi-weekly] for all services rendered during the preceding period.

2.2 Parent-Teacher Compensation

Parent-Teachers will receive tuition credits for their children enrolled in Curriculum Social as follows:

  • 8 teaching hours per week: 100% tuition waiver for one child
  • 4 teaching hours per week: 50% tuition reduction for one child
  • Additional teaching hours beyond tuition offset will be compensated at $[15-20] per hour

Parent-Teachers are also eligible for the same performance bonuses as External Teachers.

3. CONTENT OWNERSHIP

3.1 The Parties agree to the following regarding intellectual property:

  • Company-Owned Materials: All curriculum frameworks, platform-specific materials, and content developed specifically for Curriculum Social will remain the property of the Company.
  • Teacher-Owned Materials: Personal teaching materials, methodologies, and resources developed by Teacher prior to this engagement remain Teacher's property.
  • Co-Created Materials: Materials developed by Teacher during the engagement that build on Company frameworks will be jointly owned, with both Parties having usage rights.

3.2 Teacher grants Curriculum Social a non-exclusive license to use Teacher-Owned Materials for educational purposes within the platform during the term of this Agreement.

3.3 Upon termination, Teacher may continue to use Co-Created Materials for personal teaching purposes, but not in competition with Curriculum Social.

4. CHILD SAFETY PROTOCOLS

4.1 Teacher agrees to strictly adhere to Curriculum Social's child safety protocols, including but not limited to:

  • Two-Adult Policy: All educational activities must have at least two adults present at all times.
  • Visibility Requirements: Educational activities must take place in designated learning zones with appropriate visibility.
  • Communication Guidelines: All digital communications with students must occur through approved platform channels that include parent visibility.
  • Incident Reporting: Any safety concerns or incidents must be reported immediately according to Company protocols.

4.2 Teacher acknowledges that violation of child safety protocols is grounds for immediate termination of this Agreement.

5. BACKGROUND CHECK REQUIREMENTS

5.1 Teacher agrees to submit to the following verification procedures as a condition of this Agreement:

  • Comprehensive background check, including criminal history verification
  • Identity verification and right to work verification
  • Reference checks from previous educational positions
  • Verification of educational credentials and certifications

5.2 Teacher agrees to promptly disclose any changes in status that would affect the results of these verifications.

5.3 Background checks will be renewed annually or as required by applicable regulations.

6. SCHEDULING COMMITMENTS

6.1 Teacher commits to:

  • Providing availability information on a [monthly] basis
  • Honoring all scheduled teaching commitments or providing at least [48 hours] notice for cancellations
  • Finding appropriate substitute coverage for any unavoidable absences, in coordination with Curriculum Social
  • Participating in quarterly planning sessions for curriculum development

6.2 Curriculum Social will provide scheduling tools and support to facilitate efficient scheduling and will notify Teacher of any schedule changes at least [48 hours] in advance except in emergencies.

7. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION PROCESS

7.1 Teacher performance will be evaluated through a multi-source feedback system:

  • Age-appropriate student feedback collected after sessions
  • Parent feedback and satisfaction measurements
  • Peer observation and professional development feedback
  • Student progress and engagement metrics
  • Adherence to educational approach and community values

7.2 Formal evaluations will be conducted [quarterly], with ongoing informal feedback.

7.3 Teachers will receive full transparency into their evaluation data and will have the opportunity to respond to feedback.

7.4 Performance evaluations will inform compensation adjustments, professional development opportunities, and continuation of the Agreement.

8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial period of [6 months], after which it will automatically renew for successive [3-month] periods unless terminated.

8.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement:

  • Without cause by providing [30 days] written notice to the other Party
  • Immediately for material breach of this Agreement, including but not limited to violation of child safety protocols
  • Immediately if the other Party becomes insolvent, bankrupt, or enters receivership

8.3 Upon termination, Teacher will return all Company materials and complete any necessary knowledge transfer to ensure continuity of education for students.

9. CONFIDENTIALITY

9.1 During and after the term of this Agreement, Teacher agrees to:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality regarding student information and personal data
  • Protect Curriculum Social's proprietary business information, methodologies, and strategies
  • Use confidential information solely for the purpose of providing Services
  • Return all confidential materials upon termination
  • Continue to protect confidential information after termination

9.2 This confidentiality obligation does not prevent Teacher from using general teaching skills and knowledge acquired during the engagement or information that becomes publicly available through no fault of Teacher.

10. APPLICABLE LAW

10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [Jurisdiction], without regard to conflict of law principles.

10.2 Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall first be addressed through good faith negotiation between the Parties. If resolution cannot be reached, disputes shall be submitted to mediation before any legal action is taken.

10.3 If mediation is unsuccessful, any legal proceedings shall be brought exclusively in the courts of [Jurisdiction].

11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, or negotiations. This Agreement may only be modified by a written document signed by both Parties.
